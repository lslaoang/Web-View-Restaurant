Hiraya or Haraya is an ancient Filipino term for “Sana” or “I hope”; it can also mean fruit of one’s imagination, faith, wishes or dreams . In Japan, Hiraya or 平屋 means bungalow or a small or 1-storey home.


The word Hiraya is a perfect word to describe the café as it depicts the structure of a small home in Japan that is made of wood. Furthermore, the creation of the enterprise is out of the creative imagination and greatest hopes of the owners that they will be able to bring family and friends together.
